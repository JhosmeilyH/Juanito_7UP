About Us

We are a leading company in the Dominican beverage market. Within our portfolio we have local and international brands that are produced, marketed and distributed by our more than 2,000 collaborators and are preferred by consumers.

We inspire and contribute to the growth of our country, always with an innovative vision, respecting the legacy of what has been built over the years with our purpose as a guide: we dream big to create a future with more reasons to give.

At Juanito 7UP, we multiply the support we receive from our clients and consumers by creating opportunities for other small business.

The generation of jobs, the support for art, our commitment to sustainability, the investments we make and our tax contributions are the basis of the positive impact we generate for thousands of Dominican families.
